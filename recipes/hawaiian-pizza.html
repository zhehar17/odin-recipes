Hawaiian Pizza

Description

A quick, smoky Hawaiian pizza, complete with a touch of pineapple.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 slices bacon
  • 3/4 cup pineapple chunks
  • 12 inch pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 4 slices Canadian bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F. Oil a rimmed baking sheet (or a pizza pan) with olive oil.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add bacon, and cook until golden-brown and crisp.
  3. Pour out pan drippings, and cook pineapple until lightly brown. Chop bacon, Canadian bacon, and pineapple into 3/4 inch pieces.
  4. Roll dough into a 12-inch circle on a lightly floured surface. Place onto oiled baking sheet (or pan). Prick dough with a fork to reduce air bubbles.
  5. Spread sauce evenly over dough, leaving 1 inch border for crust. Evenly sprinkle cheese, bacon, pineapple, Canadian bacon over sauced area.
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes until crust is golden-brown. Wait 5 minutes before serving.
