Description

Tasty tomato sauce that's perfect for pastas, lasagnas, and pizzas.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 4 pounds peeled and chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • 1 tbsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat olive oil. Add onion and garlic powder, and cook while stirring until onion is translucent.
  2. Add tomatoes, sugar, basil, parsley, and salt. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer until sauce thickens. Make sure to stir occasionally. This will take 1-2 hours.
  4. Remove from skillet, and serve hot!
