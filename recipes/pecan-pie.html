Description

An easy, small pecan pie!

Ingredients

  • 1 6 inch unbaked pie crust
  • 1 1/2 beaten large eggs
  • 1/2 cup dark corn syrup
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/8 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup pecan halves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 Â°F.
  2. Beat eggs, corn syrup, sugar, butter, and vanilla extract together until well mixed.
  3. Arrange the pecan halves in the pie crust.
  4. Pour the mixture over the pecan halves.
  5. Bake for about one hour, until a knife inserted an inch from the crust comes out clean.
  6. Allow for cooling before serving.
